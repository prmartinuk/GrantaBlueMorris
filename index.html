Granta Blue Morris is a mixed gender Cotswold side based in Cambridge in the UK.

We were formed in 2010 as a University of Cambridge society by a small group of friends wanting to have a young and energetic Cotswold Morris side where they could all dance together.

Although the founding members are no longer students the side still keeps dancing enthusiastically and energetically, and welcome both students and non-students who are keen to dance and play.

As well as summer's evenings spent dancing at pubs, we have also displayed at prestigious events such as Ely Folk Festival, St.Neots Folk Festival, St.Albans Day of Dance and in pre show display for the Demon Barbers Lock In.

We are always looking for new dancers and musicians please see the Join Us page for more information.
