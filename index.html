Welcome to the virtual home of Granta Blue

Granta Blue Morris is a mixed gender Cotswold side based in Cambridge in the UK.

We were formed out of the desire of a small group of friends to have a young and energetic Cotswold Morris side attached as a society to the University of Cambridge.

Although Granta Blue are no longer an affiliated society to the University, we are still Cambridge's youngest Cotswold side both in terms of average age and having formed in 2010.

As well as summer's evenings spent dancing at pubs, we have also displayed at prestigious events such as Ely Folk Festival, St.Neots Folk Festival, St.Albans Day of Dance and in pre show display for the Demon Barbers Lock In.

Out of dance season, we meet on Tuesday evenings at Girton Glebe Primary School, Girton, Cambridge. Please contact us for practice details and you shall be more than welcome to join.
