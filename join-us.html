Join Us

We are always on the look out for new dancers, both male and female, as well as musicians. Don't worry if you have never danced Cotswold before, we'll teach you everything you need to know. If you are an experienced Cotswold dancer, that's even better, as you can teach us stuff we don't know!

Between September and May, practices are held once a week on Tuesday evenings at Girton Glebe Primary School in Girton, Cambridge between 6:30pm and 8pm. It is worth dropping us a message if you are interested in coming along as the school can be a bit of a maze, email us or drop us a message on Facebook

So, if you would like to join us please come along for a lot of fun, exercise, cake, dancing, big sticks and the chance to dance at festivals.
